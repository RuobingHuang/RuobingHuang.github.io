Ruobing Huang

Ruobing Huang is a PhD candidate in the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). She joined PolyU in Spring 2023.

She received both her BEng and MSc degrees from the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University. Before joining PolyU, she worked as an Image Algorithm Engineer at Rockchip Electronics.

Research Interests: Human-centered design · Human-AI Teaming · Adaptive Cognitive support · Human factors

About me

I am currently a PhD candidate at PolyU AAE. My research interests lie in human-centered AI, adaptive support, and human–AI teaming for complex and safety-critical systems, with a particular focus on aviation.
